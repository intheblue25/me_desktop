MedicEdge Usage

Algorithm Directed Troop Medical Care

A guide for patient screening

Guidance published by the United States Army Medical Command as of November 2019, MEDCOM Pamphlet 40-7-21, and reproduced in this tool with MEDCOM permission.

This software tool serves as a guide to provide commanders of military treatment facilities (MTFs), with the advice of the supervising medical director and Installation Director of Health Services, standardized clinical algorithms for use by screening personnel in determining the urgency and level of care that active duty (AD) Soldiers require and, when appropriate, in providing minor treatment to them.
